Vision

About Us

Institute for Future is an independent, public-interest non-profit organization committed to preparing Nepal and the world for the challenges and opportunities of tomorrow. Our central mission is to provide rigorous foresight analysis that empowers governments, organizations, and communities to anticipate change, make informed decisions, and build a resilient, sustainable future.

Economy

In-depth research and analysis on economic trends, market dynamics, labor trends, and financial resilience to support inclusive growth.

Our Approach

At the Institute for Future, we combine multidisciplinary research, scenario planning, and evidence-based foresight to deliver actionable insights. We engage with a diverse network of experts, stakeholders, and communities to ensure our work is inclusive, relevant, and impactful. Through publications, events, and capacity-building initiatives, we share knowledge and foster dialogue on critical future-oriented issues.

Our Commitment

As an independent, public-interest non-profit, we are dedicated to integrity, transparency, and social responsibility. We strive to be a catalyst for positive change and help turn foresight into action.
